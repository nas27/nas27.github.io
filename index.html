About Me:

I've always been drawn to technology since I can remember. As a young child, the power of instant messaging/video chatting gave me a way to connect to my friends in ways I wasn't able to due to geographical constraints. I remember hacking together a website in elementary school & being overjoyed at the potential for self expression. This led me to an undergraduate degree in New Media from Ryerson University. I learned how to think critically about the implications of all these technological advancements and express myself in a wide variety of mediums. However, something was missing. I later went to on to the Software Development & Network Engineering diploma at Sheridan. Here, I gained a scientific understanding of Information Technology to compliment my creative education. Currently working in the financial industry & graduating from my post-secondary education in 2016, I am excited for what the future holds. Here I will showcase some of my work and graciously welcome all feedback.
